Our Story

Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya, Mahe

At Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya, education extends beyond academic learning to the development of values, character, and a sense of purpose. Guided by the principle of learning with the intent to serve, the institution nurtures individuals who are thoughtful, adaptable, and socially responsible.

The residential environment fosters close collaboration among students and meaningful engagement with teachers, creating a culture of mutual respect, independence, and shared growth. These formative experiences shape not only intellectual capabilities but also life skills such as resilience, empathy, and leadership.

Over time, these foundations translate into a network of individuals connected by common experiences and enduring values. The emphasis on simplicity, inclusivity, and service continues to influence personal and professional journeys, encouraging contributions that extend beyond individual success. This platform reflects that continuing journey—bringing together people, perspectives, and experiences shaped by a shared commitment to learning, growth, and meaningful impact.

Get Involved

How Alumni
Make a Difference

Our association thrives on the active participation of every Navodayan. There are many ways to stay connected and give back.

🎓

Scholarship Fund

Support current students from economically weaker backgrounds through our alumni-funded scholarship programme.

🤝

Mentorship Network

Connect with students and guide them on career choices, higher education and professional development.

🎉

Annual Meetup

Our flagship gathering that brings alumni from across the country back to Mahe to relive memories and forge new bonds.

📚

Library Endowment

Contribute books, journals and digital resources to enrich the school library for future Navodayans.

💼

Career Fair

Alumni-led career fair offering internships and job opportunities to graduating students across disciplines.

🏆

Sports Endowment

Fund sports infrastructure and training for students to compete at national and inter-JNV levels.
